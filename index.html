There's a big need for professional commercial mortgage training. This leads to a lot of wasted time and effort on my company's part trying to sift through the loan opportunities that come our way. My idea is to create a training program thats trains brokers on 1) how to properly assess and analyze a deal 2) how to find a lender appropriate for the deal and 3) how to present a deal to the lender professionally.
ChatGPT said:

That's a solid concept, Max! It fills a clear gap in the market while addressing a pain point you've experienced firsthand. Let’s flesh this out:

Target Audience

  • Primary: Mortgage brokers, especially those new to commercial real estate lending.
  • Secondary: Professionals looking to pivot into commercial lending, real estate agents wanting to expand their expertise, or financial advisors interested in diversifying their skill set.

Offerings

  1. Foundational Training Modules

    • Deal Assessment and Analysis: Teach how to evaluate property types, borrower profiles, and market conditions.
    • Identifying Lenders: Guide brokers on matching deals to lenders' specialties and criteria.
    • Deal Presentation: Step-by-step instructions on creating professional loan packages.

  2. Advanced Modules

    • Industry Trends: Understanding the latest in lending requirements and underwriting standards.
    • Niche Lending: Focused training on fix-and-flip, ground-up construction, or mixed-use property deals.
    • Negotiation Skills: Coaching on borrower-lender interactions and terms negotiation.

  3. Certification

    • A credential (e.g., “Certified Commercial Mortgage Broker”) to boost credibility.

  4. Workshops & Case Studies

    • Hands-on experience with mock deals to apply what they’ve learned.

  5. Resources

    • Templates for loan package presentations.
    • Checklists for deal readiness.
    • A directory of active commercial lenders by specialty.

  6. Support System

    • Access to a forum or community of brokers.
    • Optional one-on-one coaching for personalized guidance.

Delivery

  • Online: Pre-recorded video modules and live webinars.
  • In-person: Workshops and boot camps in major cities.
  • Hybrid: Combine online modules with periodic live Q&A sessions.

Revenue Streams

  • Course fees (tiered pricing: foundational, advanced, all-access).
  • Subscription for ongoing resources, updates, and support.
  • Certification fees.
  • Corporate training for larger brokerages.

Marketing Strategy

  • Showcase testimonials and case studies from brokers who improved their success rates.
  • Highlight the return on investment by reducing wasted time and increasing closed deals.
  • Use your lending company’s reputation as proof of expertise.
  • Partner with industry associations and real estate groups.
